Welcome to Hogwarts

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is the finest magical institution in Britain, founded over a thousand years ago by the four greatest witches and wizards of the age: Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin.

Here, young witches and wizards learn to master their magical abilities, study ancient spells, brew powerful potions, and discover the secrets of the magical world. From Defense Against the Dark Arts to Transfiguration, from Herbology to Charms, students receive a comprehensive magical education.

The castle itself is enchanted with countless spells and filled with moving staircases, talking portraits, and hidden passages. It stands on the shores of the Great Lake, surrounded by the Forbidden Forest, creating an atmosphere of wonder and mystery.

Houses of Hogwarts

Every student is sorted into a house that reflects their true nature.

Gryffindor

Courage, bravery, and determination.

Slytherin

Ambition, cunning, and leadership.

Ravenclaw

Wisdom, creativity, and intelligence.

Hufflepuff

Loyalty, patience, and fairness.
